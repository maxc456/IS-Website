Credit Card Hacking

Lets Get Started

  1. Pick your first Card:

    • Remember treat your credit card like a debit card!
    • Always pay your card on time!
    • Never miss a payment. (Always pay in full and on time!)
    • Dont increase spending just because you have credit!

    It's important to check your credit score regularly to understand your financial health. Experian offers a free service to check your credit score. Keeping track of your credit score can help you make informed decisions and stay on top of your finances.

    Experian

  2. Points or Cashback?

    3. Do you prefer cashback or points to spend towards travel? Nerd Wallet is a great resource to use! Cashback earns real cash that is paid back to you from your spending. Travel Points can be accumulated and used to redeem travel rewards in the future!

    NerdWallet
